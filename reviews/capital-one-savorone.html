The "Entertainment" Category (The Secret Weapon)

Most cards give you points for travel or food. The SavorOne gives you 3% back on "Entertainment." This is a surprisingly broad category that most other banks ignore.

What counts as Entertainment?

  • Movie Theaters
  • Concerts & Music Festivals
  • Sporting Events (NFL, NBA, MLB)
  • Amusement Parks / Zoos / Aquariums
  • Bowling Alleys / Pool Halls
  • Tourist Attractions

The takeaway: If you're buying Taylor Swift tickets or taking the kids to the zoo, this is the card to use.

Who Should Get This Card?

Get it if:

  • You eat out or order delivery more than you cook.
  • You want a single card for groceries AND dining.
  • You go to concerts, movies, or sporting events.
  • You travel internationally but don't want to pay an annual fee (No Foreign Fees!).
  • You use Uber/Uber Eats.

Skip it if:

  • You want luxury travel perks (Lounge access, etc.).
  • You shop exclusively at Walmart/Target (Only 1%).
  • You have "Fair" credit (Capital One might reject you for this specific version, though they have a "Good Credit" version without the bonus).

Bottom Line

The SavorOne is, pound for pound, the best no-annual-fee card in existence. It covers the three things people actually spend money on: Food, Fun, and Groceries. It travels well because it has no foreign fees. It gives you 10% on Uber.

If you don't want to pay $250 for the Amex Gold, get this. It does 80% of the work for $0.

Our verdict: 4.9/5. Every wallet should have one.
