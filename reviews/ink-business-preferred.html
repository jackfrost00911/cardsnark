The 3x Categories (Where the Magic Happens)

This card earns 3 points per $1 on the first $150,000 spent in combined categories each year.

1. Online Advertising

If you run Facebook Ads or Google Ads, this is a goldmine. You are literally earning free vacations while you pay the Zuckerberg tax.

2. Shipping

FedEx, UPS, USPS. If you ship physical products, this card effectively gives you a 4.5% rebate (since points are worth 1.5 cents+ for travel).

3. Internet, Cable, & Phone

Pay your Verizon/AT&T/Comcast bill with this. It turns your monthly utility bills into points.

Cell Phone Protection (Underrated Perk)

If you pay your monthly cell phone bill with this card, you (and lines listed on the bill) are covered against theft or damage.

  • Coverage: Up to $600 per claim.
  • Deductible: $100.
  • Limit: 3 claims per 12 months.

The Math: AppleCare+ costs like $200. This card costs $95. Do the math.

Ink Preferred vs. Sapphire Preferred

They both cost $95. Which one do you need?

  • Get the Ink Preferred if: You want the bigger sign-up bonus (100k vs 60k) and spend money on ads/shipping. You also don't care about dining rewards.
  • Get the Sapphire Preferred if: You want 3x on Dining and Streaming, and your main spend is personal (food/travel).
  • Pro Move: Get BOTH. Combine the points into one account. Dominate the world.
