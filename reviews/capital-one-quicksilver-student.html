Quicksilver Student vs. SavorOne Student

Capital One actually has two student cards.

  • Quicksilver Student: 1.5% on everything. Best if you spend money on clothes, textbooks, and random stuff.
  • SavorOne Student: 3% on Dining, Groceries, Entertainment. Best if you spend all your money on food and fun.
  • The Winner: The SavorOne Student is usually better because 3% is double 1.5%, and students spend a lot on food. But Quicksilver is better if you want simplicity.

Who Should Get This Card?

Get it if:

  • You are planning to study or travel abroad.
  • You want a simple card that earns more than 1%.
  • You use Uber or Uber Eats frequently.

Skip it if:

  • You spend mostly on Dining/Groceries (Get the SavorOne Student instead).
  • You have zero credit history (Discover is easier to get approved for).
  • You want a huge sign-up bonus.
