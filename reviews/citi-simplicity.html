Here is how you use this card correctly. Do not use it to buy groceries. Do not use it to buy dinner.

No Late Fees? Really?

Yes, really. This is Citi's unique selling point for the Simplicity.

If you forget to pay your bill on the due date, most banks will slap you with a $40 fee and potentially raise your interest rate. Citi Simplicity won't.

Important Note: Just because there is no fee doesn't mean there are no consequences. If you miss a payment by 30 days, they will still report it to the credit bureaus, and your score will tank. "No Late Fees" saves your wallet, not your credit score. Pay on time.

Who Should Get This Card?

Get it if:

  • You have existing credit card debt on other cards.
  • You need a long runway (21 months) to pay it off.
  • You sometimes forget to pay bills on the exact due date.

Skip it if:

  • You pay your balance in full every month (This card earns $0 rewards).
  • You want cash back or travel points.
  • You are looking for a sign-up bonus.

Bottom Line

The Citi Simplicity is boring, unsexy, and has zero perks. And that makes it perfect. It has one job: saving you money on interest while you dig yourself out of a hole. It does that job better than almost any other card on the planet.
