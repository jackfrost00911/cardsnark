Capital One Spark Cash Plus Review 2025

For business owners who spend $50,000 a month and don't want to think about it.

4.7/5

OUR RATING (Best for Big Spenders)

The Capital One Spark Cash Plus is not a normal credit card. It is a charge card. That means it has no pre-set spending limit (great for buying inventory), but you have to pay it off in full every month (no carrying a balance). In exchange for that discipline, Capital One gives you unlimited 2% cash back on every single purchase. No categories. No caps. Just a firehose of cash back.

Charge Card vs. Credit Card

This is the most important distinction.

A Credit Card has a limit (e.g., $10,000) and lets you pay minimums.
A Charge Card (like this one) has no hard limit but requires full payment every month.

This is for businesses with high cash flow. If you run a construction company, an e-commerce store, or a consultancy where you spend $50k+ a month and pay it off immediately, this card is a dream. If you are struggling with cash flow, this card is a nightmare.

The "Personal Credit" Problem

This is Capital One's biggest flaw for business owners. Most business cards (Chase Ink, Amex Business) do not show up on your personal credit report unless you default.

Capital One DOES report to your personal credit. If you run up a $40,000 balance on this card for your business, your personal credit score might drop because it looks like YOU personally owe $40,000. Keep this in mind if you plan to apply for a mortgage soon.

Who Should Get This Card?

Get it if:

  • Your business spends over $150,000 a year (Fee becomes $0).
  • You need to make purchases larger than a typical credit limit allows.
  • You want simple cash back instead of confusing travel points.

Skip it if:

  • You spend less than $150k/year (Get the no-fee Spark Cash Select instead).
  • You need to carry a balance sometimes (Get the Chase Ink Unlimited).
  • You are protecting your personal credit utilization ratio.

Bottom Line

The Spark Cash Plus is a power tool for big spenders. Unlimited 2% back on massive transaction volumes adds up incredibly fast. If your business has the cash flow to support it, this card is a profit machine.

Our verdict: 4.7/5. The ultimate "set it and forget it" card for high-revenue businesses.
