How to Not Ruin Your Life by Age 22

Banks are not giving you these cards because they like you. They are giving them to you because they hope you'll mess up, carry a balance, and pay them 29% interest for the next decade.

The "Don't Be An Idiot" Checklist

  • Rule #1: Never spend money you don't currently have in your checking account. Credit cards are for spending money, not borrowing money.
  • Rule #2: Set up Auto-Pay for the "Statement Balance." Not the "Minimum Payment." The minimum payment is a trap designed to keep you in debt forever.
  • Rule #3: Keep your utilization low. If your limit is $1,000, try not to have a balance higher than $300 when the statement closes. It helps your score grow faster.
