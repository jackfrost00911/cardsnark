The Myth of the "Premium" Card

Marketing departments want you to believe that "serious" adults pay annual fees. They dangle shiny metal cards and "concierge service" (which is just a Google search) to get you to cough up $695.

The truth? Unless you travel 4+ times a year or are addicted to airport lounges, you probably don't need a fee card.

The "No-Fee Trinity" Strategy

You can build a powerful wallet using only free cards:

  • Card 1 (The Earner): Citi Double Cash (2% on everything).
  • Card 2 (The Diner): Capital One Savor (3% on food).
  • Card 3 (The Traveler): Wells Fargo Autograph (3x on travel, no foreign fees).
