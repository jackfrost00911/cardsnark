FAQ: Questions People Ask

Cash back vs. points?

Cash back is simple. Points are flexible but complicated. Pick based on your tolerance for optimization.

Should I carry a balance to earn more?

God, no. Credit card interest (20-30% APR) destroys any rewards. Pay in full monthly or focus on debt first.

How many cards should I have?

2-4. One flat-rate, one-two category cards, maybe a backup. More than that is overkill.

Will applications hurt my credit?

Temporarily (~5 points per inquiry). Space applications 3+ months apart. Score recovers quickly.
