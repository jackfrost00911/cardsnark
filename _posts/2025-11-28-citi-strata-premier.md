    <h2>The Deep Dive</h2>
    <p>The Citi Strata Premier (formerly the Citi Premier) is the most underrated card on the market. It doesn't get as much hype as Chase or Amex because it's not "sexy." But look at the math: 3x on Gas, Grocery, Dining, and Travel covers 80% of most people's spending.</p>

    <h3>The Math (Does it add up?)</h3>
    <p>If you take one trip a year and book a hotel through Citi, they literally pay you $5 to keep this card.</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Annual Fee:</strong> $95.</li>
        <li><strong>Hotel Credit:</strong> -$100 (If you book a $500+ stay via Citi).</li>
        <li><strong>Net Annual Cost:</strong> <strong style="color: #0c4a6e;">-$5 (Profit)</strong></li>
    </ul>

    <h3>The "Gas Station" Advantage</h3>
    <p>This is the killer feature. The Chase Sapphire Preferred earns 0x on gas. The Amex Gold earns 0x on gas. The Citi Strata Premier earns <strong>3x on Gas Stations</strong> (and EV charging). If you commute or enjoy road trips, this card fits your wallet better than the competition.</p>

    <h3>The "Citi Trifecta" (Power User Move)</h3>

    <h3>Who Should Get This Card?</h3>
