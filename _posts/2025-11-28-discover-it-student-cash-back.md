## The Deep Dive

Discover itÂ® Student Cash Back is a full-featured rewards card disguised as a starter card. Getting approved with no credit history is rare; getting approved AND earning 5% cash back is unheard of.

### The "Cash Back Match" (Why Year 1 is Insane)
This is Discover's secret weapon. Instead of a small upfront bonus, they match ALL the cash back you earn in your first year.

**The Math:** If you max out the 5% categories every quarter ($6,000 spend total), you earn $300. At the end of the year, Discover gives you another $300. That means you effectively earn **10% cash back** on rotating categories in your first year.

### Get Paid for Good Grades
If your GPA is 3.0 or higher, Discover gives you a **$20 statement credit**. You can claim this every year for up to 5 years. That's $100 just for passing your classes.

### The "Rotating Categories" Explained
Every 3 months, the 5% categories change. You have to log into the app and click "Activate." Typical categories include:

* **Q1 (Jan-Mar):** Grocery Stores or Gyms.
* **Q2 (Apr-Jun):** Gas Stations or Home Improvement.
* **Q3 (Jul-Sep):** Restaurants or PayPal.
* **Q4 (Oct-Dec):** Amazon.com and Target.

### Who Should Get This Card?
1.  You are a college student (undergrad or grad).
2.  You have no credit history and want to start building it.
3.  You plan to study abroad (No foreign transaction fees!).
