    <h3>How the 2% Cash Back Actually Works</h3>
    <p>The "double cash" name comes from Citi's unique earning structure:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>1% cash back</strong> when you make a purchase.</li>
        <li><strong>1% cash back</strong> when you pay off that purchase.</li>
        <li><strong>Total:</strong> 2% cash back on everything.</li>
    </ul>
    <p><strong>Important:</strong> If you carry a balance, you only earn the first 1% until you pay it down. This card rewards responsible behavior‚Äîpay your bill in full every month and you get the full 2%.</p>
