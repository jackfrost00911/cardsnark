## The Deep Dive

The Citi Double Cash is the boring card that's actually brilliant. It does exactly one thing‚Äîearn 2% cash back on everything‚Äîand does it better than almost any other no-annual-fee card.

### How the 2% Cash Back Actually Works
The "double cash" name comes from Citi's unique earning structure:

* **1% cash back** when you make a purchase.
* **1% cash back** when you pay off that purchase.
* **Total:** 2% cash back on everything.

**Important:** If you carry a balance, you only earn the first 1% until you pay it down. This card rewards responsible behavior‚Äîpay your bill in full every month and you get the full 2%.

### Comparisons: Citi vs. The World
How does it stack up against other 2% cards?

* **vs. Wells Fargo Active Cash:** Both are 2% flat with $0 fees and 3% foreign fees. Wells Fargo adds cell phone protection ($600 coverage) if you pay your bill with the card. Citi adds better balance transfer options (18 months).
* **vs. Category Cards:** Cards like Discover it¬Æ earn 5% on rotating categories but require activation. If you value simplicity, use the Double Cash for everything. If you like maximizing, use Double Cash as your "catch-all" for non-category spend.
