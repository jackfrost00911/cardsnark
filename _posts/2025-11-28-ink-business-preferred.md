    <h2>The Deep Dive</h2>
    <p>For anyone who has sold a used couch on Facebook Marketplace and called it a "Logistics Company," this card is for you. It is the best way to jumpstart your Ultimate Rewards balance.</p>

    <h3>The Barrier to Entry</h3>
    <p>The bonus is massive (usually 90k-100k points), but the catch is the spend requirement. You must spend <strong>$8,000 in the first 3 months</strong> to get it. If you can't hit that number naturally, do not apply. We don't want you buying 8,000 rolls of toilet paper just to get points.</p>

    <h3>Do You Even Have a "Business"?</h3>
    <p>You probably qualify. You don't need an LLC or storefront. You can apply as a <strong>Sole Proprietor</strong> using your Social Security Number. Do you sell stuff on eBay? Tutor kids? Drive Uber? Freelance write? Congratulations, you are a business.</p>

    <h3>The 3x Categories (Where the Magic Happens)</h3>
    <p>This card earns 3 points per $1 on the first $150,000 spent in combined categories each year:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Online Advertising:</strong> Google/Facebook Ads (A goldmine for dropshippers).</li>
        <li><strong>Shipping:</strong> FedEx, UPS, USPS.</li>
        <li><strong>Internet, Cable, & Phone:</strong> Turns your monthly utility bills into points.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Cell Phone Protection</h3>
    <p>If you pay your monthly cell phone bill with this card, you (and employees listed on the bill) are covered against theft or damage up to $600 per claim ($100 deductible). AppleCare+ costs like $200. This card costs $95. Do the math.</p>

    <h3>Ink Preferred vs. Sapphire Preferred</h3>
    <p>They both cost $95. Get the <strong>Ink Preferred</strong> if you want the bigger bonus (100k vs 60k) and spend money on ads/shipping. Get the <strong>Sapphire Preferred</strong> if you want 3x on Dining/Streaming and your main spend is personal. Pro move: Get both and combine the points.</p>
