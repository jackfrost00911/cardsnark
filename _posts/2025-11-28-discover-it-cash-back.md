## The Deep Dive

The card that literally doubles your rewards for an entire year. Yes, you have to remember to activate quarterly 5% categories. But if you do, the math is unbeatable for a no-fee card.

### Cashback Match: The Free Money Machine
Discover matches ALL the cash back you earn in your first year. Earned $300? Discover gives you another $300 at the end of year one. There is no cap.

**Translation:** Your 5% categories become 10%, your 1% spending becomes 2%, and you effectively double your rewards for doing absolutely nothing.

### How You Earn Cash Back
Every quarter, Discover announces two 5% categories. You must activate them to earn the bonus. Typical categories include:

* **Q1 (Jan-Mar):** Grocery stores, drugstores.
* **Q2 (Apr-Jun):** Gas stations, wholesale clubs (but not Costco).
* **Q3 (Jul-Sep):** Restaurants, PayPal.
* **Q4 (Oct-Dec):** Amazon, Target, Walmart.

### First Year Math (With Cashback Match)
Let's say you spend $1,500/quarter in 5% categories ($300 cash back) and $500/month on everything else ($60 cash back). That's $360 earned. Discover doubles it to **$720 total**. That's $720 for free just for using this card.

### The Acceptance Problem
Discover is accepted at 99% of major US retailers, but there are gaps. It does **not** work at Costco. It works at most restaurants, gas stations, and online stores. Keep a Visa or Mastercard as a backup for international travel or Costco runs.

### Who Should Get This Card?
1.  First-time credit card users (easy approval + doubled rewards).
2.  Students building credit.
3.  Category optimization nerds who want to pair this with a flat-rate card.
